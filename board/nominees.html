ISM Call for Board Member Nominations

The International Spaceflight Museum is seeking visionary, engaged, and mission-driven leaders to serve on its Board of Directors.

As we expand our educational outreach, public programming, and preservation of spaceflight history, we invite nominations of individuals who are passionate about advancing science literacy, aerospace heritage, and global collaboration in space exploration.

About the Role

Board members play a critical governance role, including:

  • Providing strategic guidance and oversight
  • Supporting fundraising and partnership development
  • Serving as ambassadors for the museum’s mission
  • Contributing expertise in areas such as aerospace, science education, nonprofit leadership, finance, law, marketing, philanthropy, or technology

This is an opportunity to help shape the future of a growing institution dedicated to honoring humanity’s journey beyond Earth.

Ideal Candidates

We are seeking individuals who:

  • Demonstrate a strong commitment to STEM education and space exploration
  • Have experience in leadership, governance, or mission-driven organizations
  • Bring diverse perspectives, global awareness, and collaborative spirit
  • Are willing to actively participate in board meetings and committee work

Previous board experience is valued but not required.

Nomination Process

Nominations may be submitted by third parties or as self-nominations. Please include:

  • A brief statement of interest
  • A résumé or CV or LinkedIn profile URL
  • A summary of relevant experience and qualifications

Submissions should be sent to Kat Lemieux by notecard in-world or by email.

The International Spaceflight Museum is committed to building a board that reflects the diversity of the global community we serve.

We look forward to identifying leaders who will help steward the next chapter of our mission.
