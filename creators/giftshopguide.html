Gift Shop Guide

To sell items in the International Spaceflight Museum gift shop, follow these steps:

  1. Set all the permissions to be wide open (modify/copy/transfer).
  2. Decide what percentage of the sales you want to receive (up to 50%).
  3. Decide what permissions you want the ultimate buyer to have. We recommend "no modify/copy/no transfer" or "modify/copy/no transfer" or "no mod/no copy/transfer"
  4. Decide what sales price you recommend. The Gift Shop might change that, but it's good to know what you think.
  5. Send a full-perm copy of the object and a notecard with your decisions to ISMGiftshop Dale.

That's all! Thanks very much for your contribution to the ISM!
